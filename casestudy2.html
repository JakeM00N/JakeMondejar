FSUU Computer Studies Program Promotional

SUMMARY

This promotional video was produced for a competitive contest showcasing the FSUU Computer Studies Program. Designed to be both engaging and professional, it highlighted the program’s facilities, activities, and student experiences. The video earned first place in the competition, demonstrating its effectiveness in presenting the program in a compelling way. Beyond the contest, the university adopted the video for ongoing promotion to prospective students and the wider community, extending its impact and reach.
