Saint Joseph Cathedral Podcast Intro

SUMMARY

For the Saint Joseph Cathedral podcast, I redesigned the intro to create a more engaging and memorable opening sequence. The project involved developing synchronized visuals, polished motion graphics, and carefully selected background music to establish the podcast’s tone and strengthen its identity. The final result provided a visually cohesive and dynamic introduction that enhances the listener’s experience and aligns with the cathedral’s brand.
